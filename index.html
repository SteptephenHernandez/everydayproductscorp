ABOUT US

Founded in 1996, Everyday Products Corporation (EPC) distributes stylish and affordable home décor products to the Philippine Market under the brand names Vista and At Home.  These are distributed via the major department stores and furniture shops in the Philippines.
EPC distributes some novelty gift items as well under the brand name Qyuts! 
The company also designs, develops and distributes similar products to major department store chains for their in-house brands.
The management and staff of Everyday Products Corporation continually works to realize its vision: To offer market leading brands and exceptional products that will delight its customers.

VISION

To offer market leading brands and exceptional products and services to delight our customers.

MISSION
